Description:

Biryani is a mixed rice dish with roots in both South Asia and ancient Persia. It is known for its fragrant aroma and complex flavors, achieved by layering marinated meat, seafood, or vegetables with long-grain basmati rice and a blend of spices

Ingredients:

  • basmati rice
  • ginger-garlic paste
  • vegetables
  • ground spices such as cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, and turmeric
  • oil or ghee
