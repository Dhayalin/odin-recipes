Description:

Parotta is a layered, flaky flatbread from Southern India, particularly popular in the states of Kerala and Tamil Nadu. A distinct type of flatbread, it is made by creating and frying thin layers of dough, unlike the simpler, single-layered roti.

Ingredients:
